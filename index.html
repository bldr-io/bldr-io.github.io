Bldr

YAML Based Build System

Introducing Bldr

There are several build systems out there (Ant, Maven, Gradle, Buildr, Make, Rake, Phing), but most of them are bogged down with xml, bad documentation, or bad configuration. Bldr aims to make build systems simpler, with a very easy yaml configuration, and easy extensibility. Take a look!
